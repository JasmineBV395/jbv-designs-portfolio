About Me

My name is Jasmine Boquer-Velasco (JBV), and I am aspiring to be an illustrator and graphic designer. I graduated from Leeward Community College in Spring 2025 with my Associate’s in Digital Media Production, and I am currently working toward a Bachelor’s degree in Graphic Design.


During my time at LCC, I worked with Ka Manaʻo, the college’s student publication, as both a graphic designer and illustrator. I also created graphic design work for Student Life, including flyers, posters, and more. I hope to continue doing this kind of work as I grow professionally and help others in developing their artistic skills.


In my spare time, I enjoy listening to music, taking walks, staying up late at night, and spending time with cats.
