Padlet is a collaborative, online platform that allows users to create and share their thoughts within a bulletin board. Padlet allows users to discuss about their work or thoughts while interacting with other users.

Write feedback for the website, post questions or anything involving your design skills, give advice and encouragement to other artists and designers, do it all!

You can post messages anonymously. However, please remain appropriate and respectful when posting them or interacting with other users.

Write a message and help make a positive difference to someone!
